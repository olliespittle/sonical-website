    Sonical builds CosmOS, the operating system for the ears.

    The problem

    Every audio device ships frozen in time.

    When you buy a new audio device, that’s all it will ever do. The hardware could do more. The software is locked.

    455Maudio devices shipped in 2024Canalys
    2B+people live with a hearing condition todayWHO / JAMA Neurology
    2.5Bpeople projected to have hearing loss by 2050WHO

    The solution

    CosmOS turns any audio device into a computer for your ears.

    One operating system for everything your device can hear and sense. Install apps, chain them together, and change what your device does years after you bought it. We call them personal hearing computers.

    How it works

    Apps, but for your ears.

    We have apps for our phones. Why should they stop there? Developers publish audio apps to the cloud. They download through the phone you already own, onto the CosmOS device in your ears. No new phone, nothing extra in between.

    The platform

    One OS. Every audio device.

    CosmOS runs on the device itself - audio, sensors and every data stream the hardware carries. Real-time scheduling, multiple apps processing the same stream, and latency low enough for professional monitoring.

    The ecosystem

    Four players. One ecosystem.

    Sonical is the technology company at the center: we license the OS, the apps platform and the reference designs. Our partners build the products.

    Each new app makes every device worth more. Each new device gives developers a bigger audience.

    Technology suppliersput chips, sensors and radios in front of every builder
    Developersbuild audio apps once, run them on any CosmOS device
    Product makersship devices that get better after they leave the factory
    Listenerschoose the apps that match their ears and their work
